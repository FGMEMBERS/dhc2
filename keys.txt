 
Keys for 3D cockpit switches:
 
 f :fuel tank select switch
 ! (shift 1): Battery switch
 @ (shift 2): Alternator switch
 # (shift 3): Fuel pump switch
 $ (shift 4 ): Pitot Heat 
 % (shift 5 ): Landing Light
 ^ (shift 6 ) : Ext lights
 
 Cargo weight can be adjusted in the internal properties /yasim/cargo
